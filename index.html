Hello!

I'm Dr. Eddie Ross. I'm a creative and curious person based in Manchester, UK. My background is in Astrophysics (research focus on the physics of the Sun and the solar activity cycle) and Space Systems Engineering. I currently work as Senior Systems Engineer at the Thales UK.

When I'm not doing science, engineering, of technology innovation, I'm busy pursuing my passion as a creative. I have developed the PHYART@UoB website, produced a podcast for the University of Birmingham careers services. In my own time I play drums in a band, called harico. I also enjoy watching live music, movies, football, and doing yoga.
