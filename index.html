Hello!

I'm Eddie. I'm a final year PhD student at the University of Birmingham, UK. My research focuses on the physics of the Sun and the solar activity cycle.

When I'm not doing science, I'm busy pursuing my passion as a creative/designer. That is either: developing the PHYART@UoB website, producing a podcast for the University careers services, or other design studies to extend my skill-set. In my own time I play drums in a band, called harico. I also enjoy watching movies, doing yoga, and watching football.
